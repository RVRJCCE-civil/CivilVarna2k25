Digital Poster Presentation Details

Rules & Guidelines

  • **Team Size:** 1 to 2 members.
  • **Topics:** Must relate to contemporary Civil Engineering challenges (e.g., Smart Cities, Disaster Management, Green Buildings).
  • **Submission:** Posters must be submitted in PDF format (A1 size, vertical orientation) via the registration link by **15-01-2025**.
  • **Presentation:** Selected teams will be invited to present their digital posters (5 minutes presentation + 3 minutes Q&A) on event day.
  • **Judging Criteria:** Focus on originality, visual appeal, content quality, and clarity of presentation.
  • **Medium:** Presentation devices (laptops/projectors) will be provided.
