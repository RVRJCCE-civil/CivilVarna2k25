Event Description

Civil Digi-Expo is a digital Poster presentation contest, an art of communication that is required for every engineer in delivering the technical knowledge. Participants are encouraged to deliver a digital poster in the following themes:

  • Trends in Concrete Technology.
  • Green Building Technology.
  • Watershed management.
  • Application of Geosynthetics in Civil Engineering.
  • Intelligent Transport Systems.
  • Applications of GIS in Civil Engineering.
  • Low Cost Housing Technologies.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Ø Participation is limited to **Civil Engineering students** only.
  • Ø Maximum of **two participants** are allowed for each Digital Poster presentation.
  • Ø Participants are required to present the Digital Poster in a **single PDF or image format**.
  • Ø Description related to the Digital Poster is to be described at the time of presentation.
