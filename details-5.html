Civil Digi-Expo (Digital Poster Presentation) - Guidelines

A digital Poster presentation contest, an art of communication required for every engineer. Participants are encouraged to deliver a digital poster on current Civil Engineering themes.

  • Eligibility: Participation is limited to Civil Engineering students only.
  • Team Size: Maximum of two participants are allowed for each Digital Poster presentation.
  • Format: Participants are required to present the Digital Poster in a single PDF or image format.
  • Presentation: The description related to the Digital Poster is to be described at the time of presentation.
  • Themes (Examples): Trends in Concrete Technology, Green Building Technology, Intelligent Transport Systems, Applications of GIS, Low Cost Housing.
