Civil Digi-Expo: Digital Poster Presentation

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • The theme for the poster must be related to modern Civil Engineering concepts (e.g., Sustainable Infrastructure, Smart Cities, BIM, etc.).
  • Posters must be designed digitally and saved as a high-resolution image file (JPEG or PNG).
  • The presentation time for the poster is limited to 7 minutes, followed by 3 minutes for Q&A from the judges.
  • Judgement criteria include design creativity, content relevance, technical depth, and presentation clarity.

Prizes

  • Second Place: ₹2000
  • Third Place: ₹1000
