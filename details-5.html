Description: Civil Digi-Expo is a digital Poster presentation contest, an art of communication required for every engineer in delivering technical knowledge. Participants are encouraged to deliver a digital poster in the following themes:

Themes for Digital Poster

  • Trends in Concrete Technology.
  • Green Building Technology.
  • Watershed management.
  • Application of Geosynthetics in Civil Engineering.
  • Intelligent Transport Systems.
  • Applications of GIS in Civil Engineering.
  • Low Cost Housing Technologies.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participation is limited to Civil Engineering students only.
  • Maximum of **two participants** are allowed for each Digital Poster presentation.
  • Participants are required to present the Digital Poster in a single **PDF or image format**.
  • Description related to the Digital Poster is to be described at the time of presentation.
