Description: Civil Digi-Expo is a competition for creating **digital posters** on contemporary topics in Civil Engineering, such as sustainable development, advanced materials, infrastructure resilience, or AI integration in construction. The focus is on visual appeal and content quality.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participation can be **individual or a team of two**.
  • The poster must be presented in a **digital format** (e.g., PDF, PowerPoint slide, high-resolution JPEG).
  • **Topics** must be related to modern/future Civil Engineering concepts.
  • Poster Submission Deadline: **20-12-2025**.
  • Evaluation is based on novelty of the topic, clarity of presentation, data accuracy, and visual design.
