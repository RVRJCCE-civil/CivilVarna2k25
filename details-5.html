Civil Digi-Expo (Digital Poster Presentation)

Description: Civil Digi-Expo is a digital Poster presentation contest, an art of communication that is required for every engineer in delivering the technical knowledge.

Team Size: Maximum two members

No Registration Fee

Register for Civil Digi-Expo

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participation is limited to Civil Engineering student only.
  • Maximum of two participants are allowed for each Digital Poster presentation.
  • Participants are required to present Digital Poster in a single PDF or image format.
  • Description related to the Digital Poster is to be described at the time of presentation.

Presentation Themes

  • Trends in Concrete Technology.
  • Green Building Technology.
  • Watershed management.
  • Application of Geosynthetics in Civil Engineering.
  • Intelligent Transport Systems.
  • Applications of GIS in Civil Engineering.
  • Low Cost Housing Technologies.
