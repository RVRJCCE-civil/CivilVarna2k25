Technical Paper Presentation Details

Description

This event is aimed to encourage students to review latest literature, prepare a technical report, to make a presentation and to communicate the concept lucidly and to interact with a group of audience to enable them to improve their communication skills, improve their stage presentation and to work with a group.

Rules and Guidelines

  • This contest is open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas and innovative thoughts.
  • Students can submit technical papers in any specialization of civil engineering. Each paper can have a maximum of TWO authors.
  • Papers should be submitted in MS word document in Times New Roman, 12pt font. Length of the paper is restricted to six A4 size papers.
  • Author details and Soft copy of Technical paper should be submitted through Google form link: here on or before 18-12-2025. Selection will be done by Technical Committee.
  • Intimation regarding selection of papers will be sent to authors email on or before 22-12-2025.
  • Important: Authors Names, email IDs, college Name and mobile numbers should be printed on the front page of the papers.
