Event Description

This event is aimed to encourage students to review latest literature, prepare a technical report, to make a presentation and to communicate the concept lucidly and to interact with a group of audience to enable them to improve their communication skills, improve their stage presentation and to work with a group.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Ø This contest is open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas and innovative thoughts.
  • Ø Students can submit technical papers in any specialization of civil engineering. Each paper can have a maximum of **TWO authors**.
  • Ø Papers should be submitted in MS word document in **Times New Roman, 12pt font**. Length of the paper is restricted to **six A4 size papers**.
  • Ø Author details and Soft copy of Technical paper should be submitted through the Google form link.
  • Ø Intimation regarding selection of papers will be sent to author's email on or before **22-12-2025**.

Important: Authors Names, email IDs, college Name and mobile numbers should be printed on the front page of the papers.
