SAMSODHANA (Technical Paper Presentation)

Description: This event is aimed to encourage students to review latest literature, prepare a technical report, to make a presentation and to communicate the concept lucidly and to interact with a group of audience.

Team Size: Maximum TWO authors

No Registration Fee

Register for SAMSODHANA

Rules and Guidelines

  • This contest is open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas and innovative thoughts.
  • Students can submit technical papers in any specialization of civil engineering.
  • Papers should be submitted in MS word document in Times New Roman, 12pt font. Length of the paper is restricted to six A4 size papers.
  • Author details and Soft copy of Technical paper should be submitted through Google form link: Google Form Link on or before 18-12-2025.
  • Intimation regarding selection of papers will be sent to authors email on or before 22-12-2025.
