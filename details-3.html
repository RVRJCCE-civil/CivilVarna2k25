Description: This event is aimed to encourage students to review latest literature, prepare a technical report, make a presentation, communicate the concept lucidly, and interact with an audience to enable them to improve their communication skills, improve their stage presentation and to work with a group.

Themes for Technical Paper

Papers should be submitted in any specialization of Civil Engineering.

Rules and Guidelines

  • This contest is open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas and innovative thoughts.
  • Each paper can have a maximum of **TWO authors**.
  • Papers should be submitted in **MS word document** in **Times New Roman, 12pt font**. Length of the paper is restricted to **six A4 size papers**.
  • Author details and Soft copy of the Technical paper should be submitted through the provided Google form link on or before **18-12-2025**. Selection will be done by Technical Committee.
  • Intimation regarding selection of papers will be sent to authors email on or before **22-12-2025**.
  • *Important:* Authors Names, email IDs, college Name, and mobile numbers should be printed on the front page of the papers.
