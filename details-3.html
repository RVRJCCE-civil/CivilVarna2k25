Description: Samsodhana is a technical paper presentation competition. We invite students to research and present innovative ideas, case studies, or new approaches within the field of Civil Engineering.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Teams can consist of a maximum of **two members**.
  • **Abstract Submission Deadline:** 10-12-2025.
  • **Full Paper Submission Deadline:** 18-12-2025.
  • Topics must be relevant to Civil Engineering (e.g., Sustainable Construction, Smart Cities, Advanced Materials, Transportation, Water Resources).
  • Presentation time: **8 minutes** for presentation, **2 minutes** for Q&A.
  • Papers must be original and not previously presented or published.
