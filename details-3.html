SAMSODHANA (Technical Paper Presentation) - Guidelines

This event encourages students to review the latest literature, prepare a technical report, and communicate the concept lucidly, improving presentation and communication skills.

üìú Rules and Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas and innovative thoughts.
  • Topic: Papers can be submitted in any specialization of civil engineering.
  • Team Size: Each paper can have a maximum of **two authors**.
  • Format: Papers must be submitted in MS Word, Times New Roman, 12pt font.
  • Length: Length is restricted to **six A4 size papers**.
  • Submission Deadline: Submit the soft copy (through the registration link) on or before **18-12-2025**.
  • Selection Intimation: Intimation regarding selection will be sent to authors' email on or before **22-12-2025**.
