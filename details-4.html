AAKRITHIK (Model-making contest)

Description: Aakrithik tests participants' ability to translate theoretical concepts into a stable, load-bearing physical structure. Teams are challenged to design and construct a miniature bridge model using specific materials within a limited time.

Team Size: Minimum 2 members

No Registration Fee

Register for AAKRITHIK

Rules and Guidelines

  • It is a team event with a minimum of 2 members.
  • Materials allowed: Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter and Thread only (provided at the time of event).
  • Selection criteria are based on Architecture, Dead weight and Maximum load measured at 30 cm span, 11 cm width and length not less than 40 cm.
  • A maximum of 12 teams will be shortlisted by a screening test on Solid mechanics, Structural analysis and other general topics.
  • Duration of model making: 1 hr 30 Min + 10 min Grace.
  • Efficiency is assessed by the ratio of failure load to the dead weight of the structure.
