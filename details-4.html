AAKRITHIK (Model Making Contest) - Guidelines

This event tests participants' ability to translate theoretical concepts into a stable, load-bearing physical bridge structure using specific materials within a limited time.

üìú Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: A team event with a minimum of 2 members.
  • Materials Allowed: Only Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter, and Thread will be allowed and are provided at the time of the event.
  • Dimensions: The model will be judged on a 30 cm span, 11 cm width, and length not less than 40 cm.
  • Judging Criteria: Evaluation is based on Architecture, Dead weight, and Maximum load. Efficiency is assessed by the ratio of Failure Load to the Dead Weight of the structure.
  • Duration: Model making duration is 1 hour 30 minutes + 10 minutes grace time.
  • Screening: A maximum of 12 teams will be shortlisted by a screening test on topics like Solid Mechanics and Structural Analysis.
