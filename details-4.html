Description: Aakrithik challenges teams to design and construct a stable and efficient structural model (typically a **bridge**) using light materials provided (e.g., popsicle sticks, thread, glue). The model will be tested for its **Strength-to-Weight Ratio**.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Teams can consist of a maximum of **three members**.
  • Participants must use **only the materials provided** at the venue.
  • The model must adhere to the specified **span length and height** constraints (to be provided on the spot).
  • Models will be tested by applying a load until failure. The winner is determined by the highest load sustained divided by the model's self-weight (Strength-to-Weight Ratio).
  • Design plan and structural presentation will also carry weight in the final score.
