Structural Model Construction Details

Description

Aakrithik tests participants' ability to translate theoretical concepts into a stable, load-bearing physical structure. Teams are challenged to design and construct a miniature bridge model using specific materials within a limited time. The model will be judged not only on its design and aesthetics but, most importantly, on its efficiency.

Rules and Guidelines

  • It is a team event with a minimum of 2 members with an objective of fabricating bridge model of your choice in a fixed time.
  • Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter and Thread only will be allowed for the fabrication and are provided at the time of event.
  • Selection criteria are based on Architecture, Dead weight and Maximum load measured at 30 cm span, 11 cm width and length not less than 40 cm.
  • A maximum of 12 teams will be shortlisted if more teams are registered for the event by a screening test comprises of short answers/multiple choice questions in Solid mechanics, Structural analysis and other general topics in civil engineering.
  • Duration of model making - 1 hr 30 Min + 10 min Grace.
  • Failure load to the dead weight of the structure is used to assess the efficiency of model.
  • There are three prizes to win (1st, 2nd and 3rd).

Prizes

**First Prize:** ₹3000 | **Second Prize:** ₹2000 | **Third Prize:** ₹1000
