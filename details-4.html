Aakrithik: Structural Model Construction (Bridge)

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • The model must be constructed using simple materials like popsicle sticks, spaghetti, or cardboard.
  • The bridge span must adhere to the dimensions specified by the coordinators (e.g., 500 mm span).
  • Models will be judged primarily on the **Strength-to-Weight Ratio**.
  • Participants must submit a brief report detailing the design and material choice.
  • Construction must be completed within the allotted time frame on the day of the event.

  • Second Place: ₹2000
  • Third Place: ₹1000
