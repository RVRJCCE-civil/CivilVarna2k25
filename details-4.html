Description: Aakrithik tests participants' ability to translate theoretical concepts into a stable, load-bearing physical structure. Teams are challenged to design and construct a miniature bridge model using specific materials within a limited time. The model will be judged not only on its design and aesthetics but, most importantly, on its efficiency.

Rules and Guidelines

  • It is a team event with a minimum of **2 members** with an objective of fabricating a bridge model of your choice in a fixed time.
  • **Materials allowed** for fabrication (and provided at the time of event): Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter, and Thread only.
  • Selection criteria are based on Architecture, Dead weight, and Maximum load measured at: 30 cm span, 11 cm width, and length not less than 40 cm.
  • A maximum of **12 teams** will be shortlisted if more teams register, via a screening test comprising of short answers/multiple choice questions in Solid mechanics, Structural analysis, and other general topics.
  • Duration of model making: **1 hr 30 Min + 10 min Grace**.
  • Failure load to the dead weight of the structure is used to assess the efficiency of the model.
  • There are three prizes to win (1st, 2nd, and 3rd).
