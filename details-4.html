AAKRITHIK: Structural Model Making Contest (Bridge)

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • Teams must consist of a maximum of **three members**.
  • The model must be a **bridge structure** constructed using supplied materials (e.g., wooden sticks, glue).
  • Evaluation is based on aesthetics, structural stability, and the **Strength-to-Weight Ratio** (Load carried vs. Model weight).
  • Design and construction time limits will be strictly enforced.
  • The final testing will involve applying controlled loads to failure.

Prizes

  • First Place: ₹3000
  • Second Place: ₹2000
  • Third Place: ₹1000
