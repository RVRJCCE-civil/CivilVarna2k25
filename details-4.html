Structural Model Construction Details

Rules & Guidelines

  • **Team Size:** Maximum 3 members per team.
  • **Task:** Construct a bridge or truss model using materials like popsicle sticks or straws (specified in registration guidelines).
  • **Evaluation:** The model will be judged primarily on the **Strength-to-Weight Ratio**. Aesthetics and design innovation also factor in.
  • **Materials:** Participants must bring their own building materials. Tools (glue guns, cutters) will be checked for safety.
  • **Dimensions:** Specific span and depth restrictions will be provided upon registration.
  • **Loading:** Models will undergo incremental loading tests until failure.
