Technical Treasure Hunt Details (EXTERNAL STUDENTS ONLY)

Rules & Guidelines

  • **Eligibility:** Strictly **EXTERNAL STUDENTS ONLY**. RVR&JC students are not eligible to participate.
  • **Team Size:** 3 members per team.
  • **Objective:** Solve a series of technical clues related to Civil Engineering principles and locations within the campus to reach the final treasure.
  • **Format:** Fast-paced, time-bound multi-stage competition. Physical endurance and quick thinking are essential.
  • **Clues:** Clues will involve basic concepts from Surveying, Concrete Technology, and Structural Analysis.
  • **Decision:** The decision of the organizing team regarding time and location tracking is final.

Prizes

**Single Prize: ₹3000** (First team to complete the hunt)
