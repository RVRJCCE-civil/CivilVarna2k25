TECH HUNT (A Technical Treasure Hunt)

Description

Tech Hunt is an exciting treasure hunt style technical event designed to test participant’s knowledge, Problem solving skills and team work in the field of civil engineering. In tech hunt, participants work in small teams to find the treasure with the help of given clues. The team who decodes the clue and finds the treasure first will win the game. The event combines fun, logic, and technical aptitude, encouraging students to apply their class room knowledge in an engaging and interactive way.

Rules and Guidelines

  • All participants must be belong to the civil engineering department.
  • Team entries only. Maximum **two members per team**.
  • It will be completely based on technical knowledge.
  • The team who will complete the task before other teams will be selected as the winning team.
  • **The students from our college are not allowed to participate in this event.**
