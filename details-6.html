TECH. HUNT (A Treasure Hunt Event)

Description: Tech Hunt is an exciting treasure hunt style technical event designed to test participant’s knowledge, Problem solving skills and team work in the field of civil engineering. The event combines fun, logic, and technical aptitude.

Team Size: Maximum two members

No Registration Fee

Register for TECH. HUNT

Rules and Guidelines

  • All participants must be belong to the civil engineering department.
  • Team entries only (Maximum two members per team).
  • It will be completely based on technical knowledge (clues).
  • The team who will complete the task before other teams will be selected as the winning team.
  • Important: The students from our college are NOT allowed to participate in this event.
