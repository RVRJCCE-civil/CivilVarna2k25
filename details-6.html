Description: TECH HUNT is a thrilling, multi-stage **Technical Treasure Hunt** across the campus. Teams must solve a series of technical Civil Engineering riddles and clues to advance. This event is exclusive to **EXTERNAL STUDENTS** to make their campus visit memorable.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Teams can consist of a maximum of **three members**.
  • This event is **ONLY** for students from colleges other than RVR & JC College of Engineering.
  • Each clue solved will lead the team to a physical location on the campus.
  • The first team to correctly solve all clues and reach the final treasure is the winner.
  • Clues will combine general knowledge, quick thinking, and core Civil Engineering principles.
