Technical Quiz Competition Details

Rules & Guidelines

  • **Team Size:** 2 members per team.
  • **Eligibility:** Open to B.Tech students from all colleges.
  • **Format:** The contest involves two rounds:
    • **Round 1 (Screening):** A written test covering core Civil Engineering subjects. Top teams qualify.
    • **Round 2 (Final Quiz):** Stage quiz with Buzzer and Rapid-Fire segments.
  • **Scoring:** Negative marking applies only in the written test (Round 1).
  • **Decision:** The decision of the organizing committee and quiz master shall be final and binding.

Prizes

**First Prize:** ₹3000 | **Second Prize:** ₹2000 | **Third Prize:** ₹1000
