KRITHI QUEST (A Technical Quiz Competition)

Description: KRITHI QUEST (Technical Quiz) is an exciting event to explore civil engineering concepts and excellence of its legacy. Participants can enjoy the mediation of quiz, nerves, goosebumps and tranquility. Participants can win exciting prizes/participation certificate. The questions are from civil engineering domain.

Team Size: Two members (B.Tech., Civil Engineering Students)

No Registration Fee

Register for KRITHI QUEST

Rules and Guidelines

  • A team consists of two members (B.Tech., Civil Engineering Students).
  • Preliminary test will be conducted to select SIX TEAMS for final quiz event.
  • In the final quiz event, there will be 3-rounds: a) Team Question Round, b) Picture Question Round, c) Rapid Fire Round.
  • Three prizes will be awarded to the teams who secured first, second and third positions.
