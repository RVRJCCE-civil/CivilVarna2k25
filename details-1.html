Description: KRITHI QUEST (Technical Quiz) is an exciting event to explore civil engineering concepts and the excellence of its legacy. Participants can enjoy the mediation of quiz, nerves, goosebumps and tranquility. The questions are from the **civil engineering domain**.

Rules and Guidelines

  • A team consists of **two members** (B.Tech., Civil Engineering Students).
  • A **Preliminary Test** will be conducted to select **SIX TEAMS** for the final quiz event.
  • In the final quiz event, there will be 3 main rounds:
    1. **Team Question Round** (Direct questions to the team)
    2. **Picture Question Round** (Identification or analysis based on images, diagrams, or famous civil structures)
    3. **Rapid Fire Round** (Quick succession of questions against the clock)
  • Three prizes will be awarded to the teams securing first, second, and third positions.
