Description: CADCon is a challenging **AutoCAD Technical Design Competition** designed to test participants' proficiency in drafting, detailing, and interpreting civil engineering drawings using CAD software. Participants must create accurate and innovative designs based on a given problem statement or sketch.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participation is **strictly individual**.
  • Participants must use **AutoCAD** software only.
  • The problem statement (e.g., structural layout, floor plan, section detail) will be provided on the spot.
  • Judging criteria include **accuracy, adherence to standards, completeness of details**, and **time taken**.
  • Use of external resources or internet access during the competition is **prohibited**.
