CADCon (CAD Design Contest)

Description

CADCon is a technical design competition that challenges participants to showcase their creativity, accuracy, and command over AutoCAD software. This event provides a platform for budding engineers and designers to apply their drafting skills in solving real-world problems. Participants will receive an on-the-spot task or theme, such as creating a building layout, sectional view, or structural plan, within a fixed time. The competition tests the participant’s ability to produce precise, professional and innovative designs while effectively utilizing AutoCAD tools and standards. The event aims to enhance practical design skills, promote technical excellence, and encourage creative thinking in engineering drawing and computer aided design. It’s an excellent opportunity for the students to demonstrate their expertise and compete with the best minds in the field.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participation is limited to individual Civil Engineering student only.
  • Screening test may be conducted to shortlist the participants to the final round.
  • Participants are required to draw plan for the given problem statement which will be given on the spot.
  • Participants will be given **90 minutes** to complete the drawing.
  • Use of mobile phones, internet and external storage devices during the event is not allowed.
