AutoCAD Technical Design Challenge Details

Rules & Guidelines

  • **Participation:** Strictly individual.
  • **Software:** AutoCAD 2020 or higher will be provided.
  • **Rounds:** The event consists of one round.
    • Participants will be given a complex Civil Engineering drawing (e.g., plan, elevation, section) to reproduce digitally within a fixed time limit (2 hours).
  • **Evaluation:** Judging based on speed, accuracy, adherence to scale, and layer management.
  • **Prerequisites:** Participants must have basic knowledge of AutoCAD commands and standard Civil Engineering drawing practices.

Prizes

**First Prize:** ₹3000 | **Second Prize:** ₹2000 | **Third Prize:** ₹1000
