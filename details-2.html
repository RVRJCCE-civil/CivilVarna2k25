CADCon (Technical Design Competition)

Description: CADCon is a technical design competition that challenges participants to showcase their creativity, accuracy, and command over AutoCAD software. Participants will receive an on-the-spot task or theme, such as creating a building layout, sectional view, or structural plan, within a fixed time.

Team Size: Individual Civil Engineering student only

No Registration Fee

Register for CADCon

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participation is limited to individual Civil Engineering student only.
  • Screening test may be conducted to shortlist the participants to the final round.
  • Participants are required to draw plan for the given problem statement which will be given on the spot.
  • Participants will be given 90 minutes to complete the drawing.
  • Use of mobile phones, internet and external storage devices during the event is not allowed.
