CADCon: AutoCAD Design Challenge

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • Participants must be familiar with AutoCAD software.
  • A challenging drawing or design problem related to Civil Engineering structures will be provided on the spot.
  • Participants must complete the drawing/design within the stipulated time limit.
  • Judgement will be based on accuracy, time taken, detailing, and proper use of layers/scales.
  • Participants must use the software provided by the organizers.

Prizes

  • Second Place: ₹2000
