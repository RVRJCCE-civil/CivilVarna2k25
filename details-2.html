CADCon: AutoCAD Design Challenge

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • The competition is strictly **individual** based.
  • Participants must use **AutoCAD software** (version will be specified).
  • A drawing problem will be provided, and contestants must submit the drawing file (.DWG) within the allotted time.
  • Judgment will be based on accuracy, adherence to standards, and timely completion.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops with licensed AutoCAD software installed.

Prizes

  • First Place: ₹3000
  • Second Place: ₹2000
  • Third Place: ₹1000
