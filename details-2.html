CADCon (Technical Design Competition) - Guidelines

A technical design competition that challenges participants to showcase their creativity, accuracy, and command over AutoCAD software. The event tests the ability to produce precise, professional, and innovative designs.

  • Participation: Limited to individual Civil Engineering students only.

  • Task: Participants are required to draw a plan for a given problem statement, which will be provided on the spot.
  • Duration: Participants will be given 90 minutes to complete the drawing.
  • Restriction: Use of mobile phones, internet, and external storage devices during the event is strictly not allowed.
